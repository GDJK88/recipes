Brownies

Description

Rich indulgent brownie recipe that can be made in to a brookie.

Ingredients:

  • Dark Chocolate (at least 70%)
    • 250g
  • Butter
    • 250g
  • Cocoa Powder
    • 65g
  • Espresso Powder
    • 5g
  • White Sugar
    • 400g
  • Molasses Sugar
    • 100g
  • Salt
    • 2g
  • Plain Four
    • 100g
  • Eggs
      • 6 Large Eggs

Steps:

  1. Chop the cholate and add it to saucepan.
  2. Add the butter and allow the chocolate to melt.
  3. Once the chocolate has melted add cocoa & espresso powder.
  4. Add sugar to large bowl then add eggs and whip untill thick.
